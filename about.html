About Our Project - AutoElegance

Welcome to AutoElegance, an car renting service project developed by our team compring of Dheeraj, Joel, Alen and Vansh, each contributing their unique skills and expertise to bring this project to life!!



Project Overview

AutoElegance was born out of our passion for cars and our desire to create an innovative platform for car dealers and customers. We aimed to streamline the car buying and renting process, offering a seamless experience to users!



Development Process

Our project was built using a stack of cutting-edge technologies, including PHP for the backend, and HTML, CSS and JAVASCRIPT for the front end. The collaborative nature of our work allowed us to combine our skills effectively, resulting in a powerful and user-friendly application.
